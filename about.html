Our Story
2020 Founded
Our Story

Born from a Community Need

StayAhead was founded in 2020 during a time when Singapore saw an unprecedented rise in scam cases. What started as a small group of concerned citizens sharing scam alerts in community chat groups has grown into Singapore's leading scam awareness initiative.

Our founders witnessed firsthand how scams devastated families from elderly parents losing their retirement savings to young professionals falling victim to sophisticated phishing schemes. They knew that reactive measures weren't enough; Singapore needed proactive education and prevention.

Today, StayAhead operates with a dedicated team of cybersecurity experts, educators, and community volunteers, all united by one goal: ensuring every Singaporean has the knowledge and tools to protect themselves.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To create a scam-resilient Singapore through comprehensive education, cutting-edge technology, and strong community partnerships. We empower every citizen with the knowledge and tools to identify, prevent, and report scams effectively.

Our Vision

A Singapore where no one falls victim to scams where every citizen is educated, every family is protected, and every community is vigilant.

Our Promise

We pledge to remain accessible, transparent, and community-focused. All our resources are free because protection shouldn't have a price tag.

Our Values

The Principles We Stand By

02

Education Over Fear
